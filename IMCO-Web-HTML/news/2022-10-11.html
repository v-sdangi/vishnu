IMCO hosts clients and prospects at 2nd annual IMCO FORUM

TORONTO (October 11, 2022) – On October 5, 2022, IMCO hosted its second annual FORUM exploring the latest global trends and their impacts on investments and investment strategies. We were pleased to welcome our guests in person to our new office space at 16 York St.

Historian, author, and Bloomberg Opinion columnist, Dr. Niall Ferguson, delivered the keynote address, followed by a fireside chat with IMCO’s Chief Strategist, Nick Chamie. Read Dr. Ferguson’s latest Bloomberg article inspired by his conversations with IMCO’s investment team here.

The FORUM featured a panel moderated by Fred Robert, Senior Principal, Infrastructure called Opportunities in Energy Transition and the Impact on Today’s Investing Decisions. Fred was joined by Matt Mendes, CEO, Pulse Energy and Natalie Adomait, Managing Partner, Renewable Power & Transition, Brookfield.

The second panel, A Singular Approach: Understanding the Opportunities Across Public and Private Credit, was moderated by Jennifer Hartviksen, Managing Director, Global Credit and featured panelists Vivek Mathew, Senior Managing Director and Head of Asset Management and Funding, Antares Capital; Mitch Goldstein, Partner, Co-Head of Credit Group, Ares Credit Group; and Andrea DiCenso, Vice President, Portfolio Manager, Loomis Sayles & Company.

ABOUT IMCO

The Investment Management Corporation of Ontario (IMCO) manages $79 billion of assets on behalf of its clients. Designed exclusively to drive better investment outcomes for Ontario's broader public sector, IMCO operates under an independent, not-for-profit, cost recovery structure. It provides leading investment management services, including portfolio construction advice, better access to a diverse range of asset classes and sophisticated risk management capabilities. As one of Canada's largest institutional investors, we invest around the world and execute large transactions efficiently. Our scale gives clients access to a well-diversified global portfolio, including sought-after private and alternative asset classes. Follow us on LinkedIn and Twitter@imcoinvest.
