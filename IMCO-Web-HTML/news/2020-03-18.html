IMCO's response to COVID-19

Contact

Neil Murphy
Vice President, Corporate Communications

Tel: (416) 898-3917

At IMCO, we are committed to doing our part in the worldwide effort against the spread of COVID-19. Preserving the safety of our employees, as well as our clients and business partners is our number one priority.

Therefore, effective March 16, we have taken the following steps, which include social distancing, while ensuring that we can carry out our normal business activities.

  • All our employees will work remotely.
  • In line with public health recommendations, no business of any sort will be conducted from any of our office locations.
  • Our Incident Management Team, which is comprised of our senior executive group and critical specialists from around the organization, are meeting daily via teleconference to monitor, assess and respond in real time to the developing situation.
  • We are actively monitoring the global markets and managing our clientsâ€™ portfolios. We are always available to our clients, with full capabilities to execute on the investment decisions.

We will continue to focus on providing the resources, information and services our employees, clients and business partners need throughout this time.
