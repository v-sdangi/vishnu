IMCO Joins Ownership Works as Investor Partner

October 19, 2023 – IMCO is proud to be the first and only Canadian-headquartered institutional asset manager among Ownership Works' investor partners. Ownership Works is a non-profit organization that partners with companies and investors to implement broad-based employee ownership programs.

Shared ownership empowers every employee as an owner, aims to boost financial security, enhances on-the-job engagement, and fosters a more robust workplace culture. Ownership Works' vision recognizes the vital role each worker plays in a company's success and that all employees should have the opportunity to build wealth at work.

"We are proud to join Ownership Works as a founding investor partner because we recognise the power of broad-based employee ownership to drive value and create equitable, win-win outcomes by ensuring that the value created is shared with the employees who help generate it. We look forward to working with Ownership Works and the consortium they have brought together to help spread the benefits of employee ownership to more workers and businesses," said Polina Sims, Managing Director, Head of Investment Strategy, Sustainability and Asset Management at IMCO, and Pension Fund Leadership Councilmember at Ownership Works.
