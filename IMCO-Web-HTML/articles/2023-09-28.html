Financial Times Future of Asset Management North America

On September 28, 2023, President & CEO Bert Clark spoke at The Financial Times (FT) Future of Asset Management North America conference in New York. Bert was joined by Tina Byles Williams, CEO, CIO & Founder of Xponance, and David Leduc, CEO, North America, Insight Investment. The panel, "Pension fund priorities - Managing institutional portfolios through the uncertainty" discussed how pension funds are adjusting to economic challenges, the potential resurgence of long-term fixed income, shifts towards private markets, and evolving views on ESG investments.
