Brian Gibson

CFA, ICD.D, Chair

Brian Gibson was appointed Chair of the IMCO board of directors in June 2019.

Mr. Gibson has 40 years of investment experience, including as Senior Vice President at the Ontario Teachersâ€™ Pension Plan and Alberta Investment Management Corporation. During his career, he has built or restructured large investment operations and has managed large investment portfolios, including those of insurance companies, a chartered bank, pension and mutual funds, and endowments.

Mr. Gibson is a director of Samuel, Sons & Company Ltd., and he is an advisory board member of Kruger Inc. He is also the chair of the Corporate Disclosure Policy Committee of the CFA Institute.
