Fred Robert

Senior Principal, Infrastructure

Fred Robert is Senior Principal, Infrastructure at IMCO. He leads a team of professionals focused on direct infrastructure investments and oversees the active management of the direct infrastructure portfolio.

Fred has experience in virtually all sectors of infrastructure. Before joining IMCO in 2020, he was Investment Banking Director with Agentis Capital, where he built a team of infrastructure advisory professionals and expanded the firm’s footprint with a new office in Toronto. Prior to that, he was an infrastructure investment professional with PSP Investments.

He serves as a non-executive director on the board of euNetworks and Databank.

Fred holds a Master of Science in Financial Economics from HEC Montreal and a Bachelor’s degree in Economics from McGill University.
