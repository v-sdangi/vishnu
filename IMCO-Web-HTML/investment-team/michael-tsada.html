Michael Tsada

Senior Portfolio Manager, Fundamental Equities

Michael Tsada is Senior Portfolio Manager, Fundamental Equities at IMCO. His focus is on identifying and pursuing high-conviction and high-engagement opportunities including negotiated transactions for meaningful equity stakes in public and pre-public companies.

Prior to joining IMCO in 2021, Michael was a Principal in CPP Investment’s Relationship Investments group. He was responsible for sourcing, executing, and managing strategic investments of $100 million to more than $1 billion in public and pre-public companies. Before that, he was an equity research associate with UBS Securities.

Michael holds a Bachelor’s degree in Business Administration, Finance from Wilfrid Laurier University’s Lazaridis School of Business and Economics.
