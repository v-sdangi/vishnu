Nick Chamie

Senior Managing Director, Total Portfolio & Capital Markets

As IMCO’s Senior Managing Director of Total Portfolio & Capital Markets and Chief Strategist, Nick Chamie leads several top-of-the-house functions including Investment Research & Economics, Portfolio Construction & Strategic Asset Allocation, Total Portfolio Management, Fixed Income & Currency Portfolio Management and Centralized Trading.

Nick has more than 25 years of experience in portfolio management, trading, and research.

Before joining IMCO in 2020, he held senior leadership roles with leading financial institutions, including Chief Strategist and Global Head for FX Strategy and Emerging Markets Fixed Income Trading and Research at RBC Capital Markets, and Chief Investment Officer, International Wealth Management and Pensions at Scotiabank. Most recently, Nick was the Senior Portfolio Manager at the British Columbia Investment Management Corporation (BCI), where he led the foreign exchange team’s strategic hedging, tactical overlay, and overall investment strategy activities.

Nick holds a Master’s in Economics from the University of Western Ontario, a BA in Economics from Wilfrid Laurier University. He is a CFA charterholder.
