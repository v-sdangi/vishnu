Greg Yearwood

Senior Portfolio Manager, Fixed Income / Government Bonds

Greg Yearwood is Senior Portfolio Manager, Fixed Income/Government Bonds at IMCO. He is responsible for managing the money market and all government bond client allocations across the various selected maturity terms.  

Greg joined IMCO at inception in 2017. Previously, he was with the Ontario Pension Board (OPB) where he internalized the government fixed income allocation. Prior to that, Greg worked at Ontario Teachers’ Pension Plan for over 19 years where he was responsible for money market, repo, nominal bonds, inflation link bonds and fixed income derivatives.

Greg holds a Bachelor of Commerce, Sports Administration from Laurentian University. He is also a CFA and CMT charter holder.
