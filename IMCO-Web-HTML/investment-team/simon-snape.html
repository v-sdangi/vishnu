Simon Snape

Senior Portfolio Manager, Total Portfolio & Capital Markets

Simon Snape is Senior Portfolio Manager, Total Portfolio & Capital Markets. Simon is responsible for Foreign Exchange (FX) management, overseeing strategic FX hedging, FX execution and research in global currency markets.

Simon joined IMCO at its inception in 2017. Previously, he was with the Ontario Pension Board (OPB) where he was responsible for direct risk taking in G10 FX markets, strategic FX hedging and contributed to the Plan's Tactical Asset Allocation activities. Prior to OPB, Simon worked for the Ontario Teachers' Pension Plan, holding various roles in the Strategy & Asset Mix team.

Simon holds a Ph.D. in Operations Research from Newcastle University and is a CFA charter holder.
