Adrien Lucisano

Senior Portfolio Manager, Portfolio Construction

Adrien joined IMCO in 2021 as Senior Portfolio Manager, Equities and Credit. He leads the portfolio construction and completion activities while conducting research on various investment topics. Adrien has 15 years of investment and capital markets experience across various asset classes. Previously, Adrien worked with Ontario Teachers’ Pension Plan where he was responsible for multi-asset programs, through both active and passive mandates. Prior to that, he was with Société Générale in London as an Investment Strategist, developing solutions for hedge funds and asset managers for strategic and tactical asset allocations.

Adrien holds a Master’s in Political Economy from the London School of Economics and Political Science (LSE) and a Master’s in Finance from the Institut des Hautes Etudes Economiques et Commerciales Paris (INSEEC Paris). He is a CFA charter holder and an active volunteer member of the CFA Society of Toronto.
