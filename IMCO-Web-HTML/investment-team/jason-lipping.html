Jason Lipping

Senior Portfolio Manager, Portfolio Construction

As Senior Portfolio Manager, Portfolio Construction, Jason is responsible for developing and communicating strategic asset allocation advice to clients, as well as oversight of asset liability modelling and development of capital market assumptions.

Prior to joining IMCO, Jason worked at a large consulting firm, first as a pension actuary before moving into investment consulting and investment management roles. In his last role, he was responsible for the design and implementation of de-risking strategies for clients along, conducting and presenting asset liability studies to clients and providing investment strategy support to the portfolio management team.

Jason is a Fellow of the Society of Actuaries, Fellow of the Canadian Institute of Actuaries as well as a CFA and CAIA charter holder.
