Careers

A dynamic and growing organization

Our passionate and innovative team is among the best talent shaping the Canadian investment management landscape today. We are one of the fastest growing investment management firms in Canada, made up of talented professionals dedicated to serving our public-sector clients.

Our spirit is entrepreneurial, our culture is collaborative, and our talent is among the best. We are a passionate, nimble and innovative team of professionals who are helping to shape one of Canada’s largest investment managers.

At IMCO, we know that an environment that fosters diversity is the kind of environment that brings out the untapped potential of our people, and stimulates innovation and organizational growth. Our people are our most valuable assets, so we strive to create a work environment that values and cultivates employee engagement and a healthy workplace culture through internal programs, activities and employee events. We believe that people who are engaged and passionate continuously seek ways to improve our business and feel empowered to adapt to change.

Our People and Culture

The IMCO team is a group of highly experienced and motivated professionals who live the IMCO values – integrity, public purpose, respect, innovation and continuous improvement and collaboration – each and every day. This strong and capable team helps drive IMCO’s vision forward and enables us to deliver on our clients’ objectives.

Our leadership, people and culture attract and inspire the brightest minds in the business – all driven by our public purpose to put our clients first. We believe all employees contribute to the growth and success of the organization and we strive to provide a work environment where everyone is encouraged and supported to reach their highest potential.

Fostering talent and developing people are fundamental to IMCO’s success. We believe that ongoing career growth is important, so we provide training and development opportunities that ensure our people can expand their skills, knowledge and experience.

Every IMCO employee plays an important role in delivering on our mission. In return, we offer competitive compensation and total rewards, including a defined benefit pension plan and incentive plans that recognize individual contribution and performance. We reward not only what our employees do, but how they achieve their accomplishments.

Our office is in the heart of downtown Toronto with direct access to public transportation and the underground PATH pedestrian walkway system.

Career Opportunities

IMCO employees are experts in their respective fields, bringing a wealth of knowledge and experience from around the world. One of our core investment beliefs is that attracting, developing and retaining world-class talent is critical to being competitive in a global investing landscape.

We are looking for dynamic, purpose-driven individuals who can collaborate with our strong team to build a world-class investment management organization. If you are a qualified individual who would like to join our talented team, we invite you to apply.
