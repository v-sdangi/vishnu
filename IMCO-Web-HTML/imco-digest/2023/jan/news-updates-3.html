IMCO's Sustainable Investing Guideline

IMCO invests in sustainable, long-term opportunities that contribute to investment returns and have a positive impact on the environment and society. Our Sustainable Investing Guideline outlines IMCO's approach to define investments as sustainable solutions and map them to the relevant UN Sustainable Development Goals.

IMCO divides sustainable solutions into two sub-categories:

  1. Climate solutions are investments in companies where a majority of their business is derived from climate solutions activities (e.g. renewable energy, green buildings, or clean transportation); and
  2. Social solutions such as investments in companies where a majority of their business is derived from social solutions activities (e.g. access to essential services, affordable basic infrastructure, and food security and sustainable food systems).

IMCO's Sustainable Investing Guideline is available on our website.
