IMCO DIGEST  |  January 2023
 

Leadership Message



IMCO releases Five-Year Strategic Plan

First and foremost, I would like to wish everyone a happy new year!

I'm very excited for 2023 and for this month in particular, as it marks the official start of our new Five-Year Strategic Plan.

Our goal over the next five years is to move beyond foundation building and focus on becoming a great asset manager that can deliver strong investment results and success for our clients. Our mission statement reflects this approach:

We work in partnership with our clients to help them meet their financial obligations on a long-term, sustainable basis.

Our five-year strategy is a natural continuation of the many things we worked on together over the last five years, with an additional focus on our four strategic pillars:

  • Investment Excellence: We will grow our capabilities in the areas that matter the most (asset mix and total portfolio management) and offer real advantages that can generate outperformance (private assets and the energy transition);

  • Client Success: We will continue to enhance our long-term partnerships with our clients through an aligned, efficient, and proactive service model. We will also work to deepen our client relationships by being a trusted source of investment knowledge, advice and insight. Our aim is to help them better understand the evolving investment context to allow them to make the big decisions required to fulfill their roles as fiduciaries for long-term liabilities;

  • Culture Evolution: We will foster a purpose-driven culture that is nimble, empowered, innovative and caring;

  • Operational Efficiency: We will enhance our operational capabilities to achieve investment excellence and high levels of client service, in a way that is consistent with our view that costs matter because they directly impact net returns.

I am proud of this team and the tremendous creativity and hard work that allowed us to achieve what we did in the first five years. I look forward to continuing to work with you to build a great organization. Wishing you all a healthy, successful 2023.

Bert Clark
President & CEO
