IMCO Spotlight



Bert Clark speaks with the Financial Post about ESG, China and investing in 2023 

Bert Clark, CEO, spoke with Barbara Shecter of the Financial Post about IMCO's approach to ESG, investing in China and attempting to why it is not viable to try to predict the market.

Decarbonization is a long-term investment trend that will create both opportunities and setbacks for investors. One way IMCO remains focused on staying on the right side of this investment trend is by working with fossil fuel companies to help them decarbonize.

Bert shared that investing in China is a high priority for IMCO, but we are taking a cautious approach and ensuring our investments are consistent with our ESG investment beliefs. At the total portfolio level, we have a small allocation to China. IMCO invests in public assets in China, which allows us to quickly alter our exposure if required, and we do not invest in private, illiquid assets.

Looking back on some of the events and trends of the past four year  -  COVID, the war in Ukraine, and rising inflation Bert expressed that it is unrealistic to predict near term future economic or market events. Rather than calling the market, our approach is to take a point of view on trends that are unlikely to disappear in the next five or 10 years. Decarbonization is one of these trends and in the coming months IMCO will launch a strategy further invest in the energy transition.
