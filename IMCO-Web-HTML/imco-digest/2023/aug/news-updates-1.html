Sofia Assaf named IMCO's Chief Client Officer

Partnering with our clients to support the achievement of their investment objectives and enhancing the client experience are key areas of focus within IMCO's Five-Year Strategy. In recognition of the heightened focus on delivering client success within IMCO's strategic objectives and organizational vision, we are pleased to announce that Sofia Assaf has assumed the role of Chief Client Officer.

Since joining IMCO in 2018, Sofia has led a number of strategic initiatives that have laid the foundation of a client-oriented organization. In addition to her continued leadership in implementing the client success strategy, Sofia will also continue to lead new client growth activities.

"I am pleased to have the opportunity to lead and contribute to IMCO's growth as a client-focused organization, which has been made possible through the exceptional partnerships we have with our clients. As an asset management organization that is passionately committed to our clients and invested in their success, we will continue to make enhancements that support a consistent, exceptional client experience. I am also really looking forward to building strong relationships with our new clients as IMCO continues to grow." - Sofia Assaf
