IMCO acquires NeXtWind through consortium-led investment

IMCO, together with our strategic partner Sandbrook Capital and co-investor PSP Investments, have signed an agreement to acquire leading German renewable energy company NeXtWind Capital Ltd.

As part of the transaction, IMCO, Sandbrook Capital and PSP Investments have committed up to US$750 million of equity capital to NeXtWind to acquire its existing portfolio of operating wind assets and to fund future growth.

This marks IMCO's first direct investment in the German wind energy sector and reinforces our commitment to boosting global renewable energy production while further diversifying clients' portfolios.
