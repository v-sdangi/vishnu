IMCO joins the Ride to Conquer Cancer

IMCO assembled a team of eleven courageous cycling enthusiasts to participate in the Ride to Conquer Cancer.

The Ride to Conquer Cancer is Canada's largest cycling fundraiser benefiting The Princess Margaret Cancer Centre. Over two days, Team IMCO collectively cycled over 2,300 km between Toronto and Niagara Falls.

Congratulations to Team IMCO who raised over $25,000 for a great cause!
