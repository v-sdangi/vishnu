ESG report to launch September 28

IMCO will release its 2022 ESG report on September 28.

Following the success of last year's inaugural report, the 2022 ESG report will capture IMCO's ESG activities and progress over the past year, including:

  • Publishing our Climate Action Plan
  • Establishing interim climate targets for 2030
  • Making key investments in energy transition solutions
  • Implementing our DEI strategy

The report will also include our response to the recommendations of the Task Force on Climate-related Financial Disclosures (TCFD), demonstrating how we integrate climate-related risks and opportunities into our investment process.

The ESG report will be available on IMCO's website.
