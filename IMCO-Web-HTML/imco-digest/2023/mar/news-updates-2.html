News and Updates



Enhancing the client experience around financial reporting

The Target Financial Reporting and Services (FRS) model is a priority, multiyear initiative IMCO launched to ensure the timeliness and accuracy of client financial reporting in a consistent and efficient manner.

In the first phase of this initiative, we engaged with clients over the scope of financial reporting services we will provide to put IMCO in the best position to meet the ongoing needs of existing and future clients. Daily transaction processing and custodian oversight, quarterly and annual reporting on IMCO pooled investments, external audit support, and regular finance and operations meetings with clients are some of core the services that are part of the model.

Since launching the initiative, clients have observed tangible and demonstrated progress on the quality, timeliness, and accuracy of our financial reporting deliverables.

In 2023, we will continue to build on the positive momentum to further progress this initiative. The plan for 2023 includes:

Finalizing design of the target FRS model - Incorporating client input into the design of the model.

Streamlining internal processes - Identifying opportunities to standardize and simplify client reports to create more efficient processes.

Keeping clients actively engaged - Providing updates on our progress and aligning proposed changes to processes/deliverables.

IMCO is pleased to see the target FRS model has helped build trust and reliability or our financial reports. As we continue to implement the model, we are confident it will enhance services to support clients with their financial and accounting requirements.
