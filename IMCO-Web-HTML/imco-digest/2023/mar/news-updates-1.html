IMCO's SAA advice, research and ESG strategy helps drive client survey results upwards

Client focus, partnership and innovation are three of IMCO's values that we embrace as an organization to help inform how we work together with our clients. The Annual Client Survey is an important feedback mechanism that enables us to improve upon and deliver a better client experience. Close partnership with our clients and strengthening our role as a trusted advisor remains a core focus for IMCO, and client feedback is a fundamental component to enhance our capabilities.

The 2022 survey indicated that IMCO is well rounded in our corporate values, as satisfaction scores trended upwards across client and survey categories. Clients believe IMCO remains focused on understanding their unique objectives and circumstances when providing strategic asset allocation (SAA) advice. Clients also found our research material is insightful and relevant to market events. A suggested area of improvement is to link our research to the implementation of SAA advice, and to engage clients earlier on in the process when implementing strategic initiatives.

IMCO's inaugural ESG Report and the release of our interim climate targets for 2030 contributed to clients' positive impression of our ESG strategy.

IMCO will continue to innovate to address key themes identified in the survey. Ongoing client engagement and feedback is critical to delivering on our client success mandate and ensuring we are prioritizing enhancements in areas that matter most to our clients. IMCO will leverage annual client survey results and recent client dialogues to outline an annual client experience (CX) action plan to address gaps and key priorities for 2023. We will also continue to execute on the implementation plan for the client success strategic pillar and engage and update clients as key partners actively over the course of the year.
