Tanya Lai moderates panel at CPBI Ontario Economic Outlook 2023

Tanya Lai, Managing Director, Public Equities, moderated an economic outlook panel hosted by the Canadian Pension and Benefits Institute Ontario on January 25.

Tanya led a discussion that focused on geopolitical risk and inflation. According to the panel, which was comprised of Michael A. Mullaney, Director of Global Markets Research, Boston Partners; Aaron Bennett, Chief Investment Officer, University Pension Plan Ontario (UPP); and John Bai, Senior Vice President, Chief Investment Officer, NEI Investments; geopolitical risk, notably in China, is now a larger consideration in portfolio construction for institutional investors. Tanya highlighted that the Chinese government is focused on economic growth, and that consumers in China have not been deterred by inflation, unlike western economies - where inflation and a looming recession are having a noticeable impact.

Inflation has made it difficult to manage liquidity, as many investors work to balance meeting their liabilities and holding capital to fund investment opportunities. More volatility is expected in 2023 and the panel agreed that a balanced 60/40 portfolio is the best approach.
