Navigating the U.S.-China Rivalry: An IMCO Research Report

China is on pace to surpass the U.S. as the world's largest economy by the 2030s. This rise as a global superpower has brought on a changing geopolitical dynamic and ongoing tense relationship between the two countries.

IMCO examines the major macroeconomic and political dimensions of the U.S.-China relationship in our latest research report, Navigating the U.S.-China Rivalry. Through this analysis we were able to form several viewpoints to help inform our investment strategy in China.
