Leadership Message



Evolving our culture to deliver client success

At IMCO, we recognize that the continued development of our culture is essential to accomplishing the strategic objectives set out in our Five-Year Strategic Plan. Gayle Fisher, IMCO's Chief Corporate Officer, elaborates on how we are continuously evolving our culture to optimize client success.

Further strengthening IMCO's client focus continues to be a key priority within the Five-Year Strategic Plan. While many initiatives are currently underway, one notable achievement supporting this priority was the launch of a learning program focused on the foundational concepts of client success and relationship management. All IMCO employees completed this learning and the program is now embedded in our new hire onboarding, ensuring that every IMCO team member understands how they impact and influence the client experience.

We know that how well we work together as an organization and treat each other as colleagues will influence our delivery of client success. We continue to empower our teams to be nimble and pursue innovation, by providing entrepreneurial opportunities and finding creative solutions that benefit our clients and achieve optimal investment outcomes. The recent collaborative effort between our Fundamental Equities and Infrastructure groups on the Northvolt AB investment demonstrated both innovative and nimble thinking.

IMCO is committed to our people and strongly believes that engaged employees are key to delivering a consistent and positive client experience. We are proud of our positive employee engagement scores and strength in inclusivity, diversity and social well-being. Our continued focus on building a sense of purpose and recognition for IMCO employees enables us to effectively deliver on our commitment to our clients.

Gayle Fisher
Chief Corporate Officer
