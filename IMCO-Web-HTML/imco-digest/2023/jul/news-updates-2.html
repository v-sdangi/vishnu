IMCO joins peer plans in support of ISSB disclosure framework

In June, IMCO, alongside ten of Canada's leading pension plan investment managers, issued a joint statement in support of the new International Sustainability Standards Board (ISSB) disclosure framework.

IMCO has been calling for global alignment between sustainability reporting standards and we welcome the release of the ISSB standards.

Adopting the ISSB standards will establish greater consistency when disclosing the effect of climate-related risks and opportunities on a company's value. Assessing disclosure data in a common language will help IMCO evaluate ESG risks and opportunities and make more informed investment decisions, generating long-term sustainable value for our clients.
