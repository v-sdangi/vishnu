IMCO hosts first Day of Giving

On April 26 IMCO hosted its first Day of Giving, partnering with Kits for a Cause in support of the Native Women's Resource Centre of Toronto. The Centre provides a safe and welcoming environment for Indigenous women and their children in the Greater Toronto Area.

IMCO staff gathered to build over 1,400 hygiene kits for the Centre.

"The Day of Giving was a fantastic display of IMCO's great culture and our commitment to building a caring and inclusive workplace. We had an incredible turnout from staff, who put so much care and attention into assembling the kits to support a great cause." - Veneza Baptista, Senior Director, Human Resources and Talent Acquisition.
