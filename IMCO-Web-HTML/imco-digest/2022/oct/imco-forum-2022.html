IMCO Spotlight



IMCO FORUM 2022

On October 5, 2022, IMCO hosted our second annual FORUM exploring the latest global trends and their impacts on investments and investment strategies.

We were pleased to welcome our guests in person to our new office space at 16 York St. Historian, author, and Bloomberg Opinion columnist, Dr. Niall Ferguson, delivered the keynote address, followed by a fireside chat with IMCO's Chief Strategist, Nick Chamie. You can read Dr. Ferguson's latest Bloomberg article inspired by his conversations with IMCO's investment team here.

The FORUM featured a panel moderated by Fred Robert, Senior Principal, Infrastructure, called Opportunities in Energy Transition and the Impact on Today's Investing Decisions. Fred was joined by Matt Mendes, CEO, Pulse Energy; and Natalie Adomait, Managing Partner, Renewable Power & Transition, Brookfield.

The second panel, A Singular Approach: Understanding the Opportunities Across Public and Private Credit, was moderated by Jennifer Hartviksen, Managing Director, Global Credit and featured distinguished panelists Vivek Mathew, Senior Managing Director and Head of Asset Management and Funding, Antares Capital; Mitch Goldstein, Partner, Co-Head of Credit Group, Ares Credit Group; and Andrea DiCenso, Vice President, Portfolio Manager, Loomis Sayles & Company.

Visit the IMCO Insights section of our website to view both panels.
