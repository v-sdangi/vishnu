IMCO DIGEST  | November 2022
 

Leadership Message



Welcome to the first edition of the IMCO DIGEST

At IMCO, delivering client success is at the centre of our public purpose mandate. Among our measures of success is fostering a positive client experience that includes timely, relevant, and engaging communications about IMCO initiatives.

It is my pleasure to introduce you to the IMCO DIGEST! This publication will be distributed regularly to help keep you informed and "in the know" about key developments and initiatives at IMCO. It is part of our commitment to further enhancing and broadening our communication reach to our valued clients.

Each DIGEST issue will contain messages from our Leadership, "Behind the Deal" details on investment transactions, insights from our in-house teams of subject matter experts and updates on what's happening at IMCO.

The IMCO DIGEST is an additional channel of communication and will not replace any of our existing information-sharing channels and protocols. It is just one more way to keep you up to date on material developments and value-generating activities happening at IMCO. We invite you to share the DIGEST with others across your organization.

On behalf of the IMCO Client Relationship Management Team, a sincere thank you for your ongoing support and partnership. We are focused on further strengthening our relationship and partnership and ensuring an enhanced client experience.

I hope you enjoy our inaugural publication and look forward to your comments and feedback.

-Sofia Assaf
Senior Vice President, Head of Client Relationship Management
