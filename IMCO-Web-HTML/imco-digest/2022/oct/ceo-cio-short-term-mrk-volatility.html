IMCO's CEO & CIO share our view on how to manage short-term market volatility

In a recent IMCO Insights article, Bert Clark, President & CEO and Rossitsa Stoyanova, CIO, describe our approach to responding to short-term market events.

Short-term volatility is inevitable for long-term investors. As painful as these periods of underperformance can feel, we believe that investors should not attempt to tactically adjust their asset mix to avoid short-term weakness in returns. Instead, investors should remain committed to their long-term asset mix and seek out opportunities that can reduce risk and enhance returns.
