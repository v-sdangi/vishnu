Matthew Mendes joins IMCO as Managing Director, Head of Infrastructure

We are pleased to announce that Matthew Mendes will join IMCO as the new Managing Director, Head of Infrastructure.

Matt joins us from Pulse Clean Energy, a UK leader in transitioning energy networks to renewables via battery storage, where he served as CEO since 2021. As many of you know, Pulse Clean Energy is the first company IMCO fully acquired, and we are delighted to grow our relationship with Matt. Over the course of his 15+ year career, Matt has executed investments involving onshore and offshore wind and solar projects, tax equity financing, corporate power purchase agreements, and energy production hedging. In total, he has been involved in the execution of more than $100 billion of corporate M&A transactions.

Matt brings deep expertise in global power markets, and a track record of sourcing infrastructure opportunities and growing them from investment thesis to operational leaders across diverse sectors.

His previous roles include senior positions at Whipcord Ltd. and Enbridge.

We look forward to welcoming Matt to IMCO on November 14.
