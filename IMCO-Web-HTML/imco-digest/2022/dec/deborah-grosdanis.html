Deborah Grosdanis joins IMCO as VP, Client Relationship Management (CRM)

As we continue to strengthen IMCO's CRM team and deliver on client success, we are pleased to announce that Deborah Grosdanis has joined as Vice President, CRM on December 1.

Deborah brings to the team 15 years of transaction and market experience specializing in client advocacy and global operational excellence. At IMCO she will lead initiatives to optimize client relationships and improve reporting and service. She will also participate in key client deliverables, so we hope you get to meet her soon.

"I look forward to collaborating with our clients to achieve our shared objectives, further strengthening our partnership, and enriching the overall client experience." - Deborah Grosdanis, VP CRM

Prior to joining IMCO, Deborah served as the Director, Sales Effectiveness, Global Business Payments at Scotiabank. Deborah holds a BBA from the University of Toronto and an MBA from the Schulich School of Business. She is also Co-Chair of the Steering Committee Board of Women in Capital Markets (WCM).
