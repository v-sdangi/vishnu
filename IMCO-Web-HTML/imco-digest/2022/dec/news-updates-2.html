IMCO publishes Stewardship Guideline and endorses ICGN and CCGG

IMCO believes that stewardship helps us better manage risk and contributes to long-term sustainable value. We aim to use our influence to promote strong ESG practices, whether through direct engagement with portfolio companies and external managers, proxy voting or collaborative engagement with our peers.

As a signatory to the Principles for Responsible Investment, IMCO supports Active Ownership 2.0 which focuses on:

  • Outcomes seen in the real world
  • Outcomes that benefit economies and societies as a whole, and
  • Collaboration with other investors and service providers

In November, IMCO released our Stewardship Guideline that describes our approach for engaging with entities in which we invest on material ESG issues. Throughout stewardship activities, we seek to influence portfolio companies and external managers to adopt sustainable business practices.

IMCO is a member of the International Corporate Governance Network ("ICGN") and the Canadian Coalition for Good Governance ("CCGG"). We endorse both of these organizations and align our stewardship approach with them.

"ICGN is delighted to welcome Investment Management Corporation of Ontario (IMCO) as an Endorser of ICGN Global Stewardship Principles. As regulators and clients demand increased transparency and accountability for responsible investment practices, public alignment with globally accepted stewardship standards is more important than ever. IMCO is clearly demonstrating this as an active and valued ICGN Member." - Kerrie Waring, Chief Executive Officer, ICGN

IMCO's Stewardship Guideline is available on our website.
