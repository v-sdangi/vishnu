IMCO's World View examines global trends and the impact to clients' portfolios

After decades of relative global stability and 'predictable' economic policy, the world appears to be shifting towards a more volatile regime. At IMCO, we believe that global trends are leading us towards a new era of investing - one where it becomes increasingly difficult to rely on the past as a predictor of the future.

IMCO's inaugural World View represents our efforts to define and understand the trends that will have the most impact on our clients' portfolios. We explore the underlying themes that we expect will play a significant role in driving asset class returns over the coming decade and beyond. We also examine the economic and market implications of these themes and what IMCO can do in response to these outcomes.

Our World View serves as a starting point of a new research effort to help us better navigate the changes we see taking place, and to shape our forward-looking views that will enable us to better serve our clients through our asset mix advice and investment strategies.
